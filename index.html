Services

  • Competency Evaluation / Fitness to Proceed

    Assessment of a defendant's present ability to understand the nature of proceedings and meaningfully assist counsel, consistent with Dusky v. United States. Available to defense and prosecution in Montana, Colorado, and New York.

  • Psychiatric Evaluation / Diagnosis Clarification

    Independent evaluation to establish, clarify, or challenge psychiatric diagnoses in the context of criminal or civil proceedings, disability determinations, or court-ordered assessment.

  • Mental State Evaluation / Criminal Responsibility

    Retrospective analysis of a defendant's mental state at the time of the alleged offense, including Not Guilty by Reason of Insanity (NGRI) and diminished capacity evaluations.

  • Record Review

    Systematic review of medical, psychiatric, correctional, and collateral records to identify clinically significant findings and support expert opinions in litigation.

  • Treatment Recommendations

    Evidence-based recommendations addressing restoration to competency, court-ordered treatment compliance, and forensic monitoring in inpatient or community settings.

  • Sentence Mitigation

    Psychiatric evaluation addressing diagnoses, developmental history, trauma, and neurocognitive factors relevant to sentencing, including capital and enhanced sentencing matters.

About

Dr. Bemporad brings extensive forensic psychiatry experience developed as the sole full-time psychiatrist for Montana's 54-bed forensic inpatient unit. He has authored or coauthored more than 500 forensic evaluations addressing competency to stand trial (fitness to proceed), criminal responsibility, mental state at the time of offense, and sentencing mitigation. He has provided expert testimony in nearly every district court across Montana and is experienced in communicating complex psychiatric findings in clear, objective, and legally relevant terms. His background includes serving as a Forensic Review Board voting member, Chair of the Involuntary Medication Review Boards for both Montana State Hospital and the Montana State Prison, and former President of the Medical Staff of Montana State Hospital. Dr. Bemporad has presented on forensic topics to legislative commissions and state leadership, testified in over 20 Sell hearings, and routinely collaborates with attorneys, courts, and state agencies to provide thorough psychiatric consultation.

Serving Attorneys & Courts in Montana, Colorado, and New York

Dr. Bemporad accepts forensic psychiatry referrals from criminal defense attorneys, prosecutors, public defenders, and courts across Montana, Colorado, and New York. He is available for in-person or remote evaluations throughout his licensed jurisdictions. Attorneys seeking a board-certified forensic psychiatrist for competency to stand trial, criminal responsibility, or sentencing mitigation matters are encouraged to reach out directly.
